Li Lyna Zhang

I am currently a fourth-year PhD student in School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I'm in a joint PhD program between USTC and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I am very fortunate to be supervised by Dr. Chieh-Jan Mike Liang and Prof. Enhong Chen. Prior to that, I obtained my bachalor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, USTC in June, 2013.

My research interests include applying data-driven intelligence to optimize the performance and user experience of mobile systems. I enjoy solving real world problems by building real systems. Currently, I'm working on mobile app testing.
